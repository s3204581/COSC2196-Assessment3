Roles

Specified roles were not assigned to any group members at any time throughout the lifetime of our project. Utilising an agile methodology alongside tools such as our Trello board we were focused on collaboration between our members and taking on tasks within our individual knowledge and skills.

This approach allowed us to have more control over elements of the project were responsible for; to promote self-organisation and not be restricted by any titles or established roles. This methodology also promoted cross-functional collaboration, and communication between group members was paramount to outline our individual sprints. While many of our tasks could easily fall under the purview of a role such as User Interface Designer or Technical designer, without the specific designation of such a role there was no limitation to the responsibilities of the individual engaging in the task to be completed. As a result, the completion of the task in full was our measure of progress.
