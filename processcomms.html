Group Processes and Communication

Our group elected to use an MS Teams platform for our primary source of communication as it served to provide the utility to not only host and record meetings but also allow storage of documentation, real time collaboration and a calendar system to plan future meetings and notify the team of upcoming milestones. The board also allowed group members to post personal updates regarding their work or notifying some or all members of their progress and any other formal or non-formal chat along with having access to a host of other Microsoft products such as PowerPoint for presentation planning and Excel for templates and spreadsheets. The forum also allowed the team to plan any further ad hoc meetings and to also access meeting minutes in the event of an absence or as a reference point to generate a future action plan.
