Team Reflection

As we conclude our project journey, we reflected on the experiences, challenges, and achievements that have shaped our collective efforts. Our project, PhishNet, has been a project of collaboration and dedication. This reflection encapsulates the lessons we've learned and the growth we've experienced as a group. Our group comprised individuals from varied backgrounds, each bringing unique perspectives and skill sets to the table. This diversity, while initially presenting challenges in communication and understanding, ultimately led to the creation of innovative and well-rounded solutions. We learned that embracing diverse viewpoints fosters creativity and widens our horizons. By pooling our skills, insights, and efforts, we achieved outcomes that far exceeded what any individual could have accomplished alone. The synergy of our collective work brought depth and breadth to our project, resulting in an outcome that we are proud to present. In conclusion, our project journey has been one of growth, learning, and accomplishment. We acknowledge the challenges we faced and the lessons we've learned as invaluable components of our experience. As we move forward individually and collectively, we carry with us the knowledge that effective collaboration, communication, and a resilient mindset are keys to success. Our achievements stand as a testament to our dedication and determination as a group.

Dave's reflection

We worked better as a group for this assignment. We tried to implement an agile sprint system using the Trello board and this helped us even if the sprints blurred into one at the end. The board was useful in a lot of respects it helped me see the full scope of what we needed to do. And it helped me stay motivated to keep working on tasks. It was also useful to see what other people were working on. We also met more frequently and communicated better. Our collaboration on specific tasks could still use some improvement. For example, I did a lot of work on the prototype and in retrospect I wish I could have collaborated with other members of the group more on it. Even though we had the board our time management still could use some work. We anticipated that we would have a lot to do in the last few days, but I wish I worked harder earlier so the end was not so stressful. The idea was a fun idea. I enjoyed researching cyber security and looking into what the cyber security market is like. It has inspired me to keep researching cyber security, I might try to work on some of my own cyber security projects in the future.
